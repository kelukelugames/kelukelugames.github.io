A Strategy for Change

I watched Obama’s commencement speech at Howard. He said passion is not enough. I need a strategy if I want to change the world. Currently I am mentoring some people through a program at work. Mentoring is helpful, and following someone else’s plan is a legitimate plan. But those things do not scale. I need to help more than a handful of people at a time. So what is my plan? My plan is to write. I have a unique voice as a 1.5 generation Chinese immigrant. Paired with writing skills and a great editor, I can tell our stories.
