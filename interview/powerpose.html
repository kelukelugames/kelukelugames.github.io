How to Power Pose before an Interview

Interview are hard. A challenging coding question can knock any smart engineer off of their “A” game. Staying mentally strong is the key to doing well. But when your confidence is slipping away, how do you recover? Strike a power pose! Look big and strong and you will feel big and strong.

But you definitely don’t want to power pose during the actual interview. Your interviewer will think you are arrogant, rude, and all around unpleasant to work with.

The good news is you can still do a power pose before the interview and ride the ego boost for the entire day. You can get an early start in the parking lot or do a classic policeman pose on the elevator ride up.
